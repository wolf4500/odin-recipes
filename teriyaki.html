Teriyaki Chicken

Tasty Japanese-style chicken best served with rice

My favorite food to meal-prep with!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Cubes
  • 1 part soy sauce
  • 1 part mirin
  • 0.5 part honey
  • Black Pepper
  • Corn Starch

Instructions

  1. Mix in proportional amounts of soy sauce, mirin, honey in a small bowl
  2. Leave sauce aside until later
  3. Sprinkle black pepper onto chicken and mix in a bowl
  4. Toss chicken in corn starch
  5. Put oil in a pan and set on medium-high heat
  6. Wait for pan to heat up
  7. Place chicken cubes in pan and wait until seared
  8. Flip chicken over and cook for 30 seconds
  9. Pour in prepared sauce and mix chicken in the sauce
  10. Let chicken cool for 3-4 minutes
  11. Place chicken on plate with rice and enjoy!
